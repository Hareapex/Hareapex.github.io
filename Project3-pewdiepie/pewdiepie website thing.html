The Games I'm Playing

A look at the classics and new picks coming to the channel. Check out what I've been playing and decide if you want to watch.

Games

Deus Ex

A landmark in first-person RPGs. You are JC Denton, a nano-augmented agent navigating a world full of conspiracies, moral choices and cyberpunk atmosphere. Every decision matters.

Dungeon Keeper

You are the villain. Build your dungeon, recruit monsters and destroy the heroes who dare invade your lair. A dark strategy classic that flips the genre on its head.

Battlefield 3

Large scale military warfare at its peak. Massive maps, destructible environments and intense multiplayer. One of the best shooters ever made — controversial opinion, but I stand by it.

Red Alert 2

The Cold War gone completely insane. Build your base, command your army and crush your enemies in one of the most beloved real-time strategy games of all time.

Black Ops II

The Call of Duty that everyone agrees was actually good. A branching storyline, futuristic setting and multiplayer that kept people hooked for years. The peak of the series.

Civilization V

One more turn. Always one more turn. Build a civilization from scratch, research technology, wage war or pursue diplomacy across thousands of years of history. Dangerously addictive.

Doom

The game that started it all. Fast, brutal and unforgiving. You are the marine, hell has invaded and your job is to shoot everything until it stops moving. A classic for a reason.

Max Payne 2

A noir tragedy wrapped in slow-motion gunfights. Max Payne 2 is short, sharp and emotionally heavy. The bullet time mechanic still holds up and the story hits harder than expected.
